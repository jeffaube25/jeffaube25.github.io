About Me

My name is Jeffrey Aube and I'm a fourth-year Management Information Systems student at the Rochester Institute of Technology with a minor in Mobile Application Development. My goal is simple - to become an expert in all areas of technology throughout my career and continually be able to advise people on how to leverage them as my knowledge and experience expand.

Too often in the tech industry do I hear about a common problem - project or product managers understand how to manage, but not the tech behind what they're managing. As someone with a colorful background, I want to use my experience and passion for technology along with my strong ability to understand and communicate with people to direct tangible change in whichever organization(s) I end up.
